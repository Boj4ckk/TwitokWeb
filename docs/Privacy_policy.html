Twitok - Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how Twitok collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Data We Collect

Twitok collects the following data:

  • Access to your Twitch and TikTok accounts for retrieving clips and posting videos.
  • Basic account information necessary for authentication (OAuth tokens).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely for the purpose of providing the Twitok service. This includes:

  • Automatically retrieving Twitch clips from your account.
  • Editing and formatting those clips for TikTok using MoviePy.
  • Uploading the formatted videos to your TikTok account.

3. Data Sharing

Twitok does not share your data with any third parties. Your data is only used to facilitate the services described above.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, we cannot guarantee complete security of data transmitted over the internet.
